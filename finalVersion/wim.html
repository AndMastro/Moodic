What is Moodic?


Moodic is a new way to experience your mood through music.

You simply select the mood you're in and our system will
turn on the right tune that suits you the best.
You can read the lyrics, look at the album cover,
read more details about the current song
or you can just live your mood without any of these.
If you particularly loved it, you can even downalod the track for FREE!

What are you waiting for? Get into your mood!
